Visit our Theme Parks

Magic Kingdom Park

Explore Lands of Endless Enchantment, Where Your Fantasy Becomes a Reality. Zoom through the galaxy, brave a ghostly mansion and embark on high-seas adventures during the Golden Age of Piracy.

Epcot Center

Travel Around the Globe, Under the Sea, into Outer Space… and Beyond! Blast off on a journey into our solar system, glide around the world, sail away on a Frozen adventure and so much more.

Hollywood Studios

Take Center Stage in the Worlds of Movies, Television, Music & Theater! Board your toy rocket and hang on tight as the Aliens take you on a spin through space.

Animal Kingdom Park

Behold the Magic of Nature with Rare Animals and World-Class Entertainment! Head to the Discovery Island Stage to kick up your heels with some popular Disney peeps.

Blizzard Beach

Chill out all year round at this 66-acre water park, built around the mythical tale of a one-time ski resort that melted into soothing, warm water—creating the perfect water playground.

Typhoon Lagoon

Delight in this tropical paradise that’s been transformed by a legendary storm into an oasis of pure excitement!
